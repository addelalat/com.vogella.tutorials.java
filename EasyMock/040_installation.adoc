[[easymock_installation]]
== Download Easy Mock
	
Download
EasyMock
from the http://easymock.org/[EasyMock Homepage]
and add the
`easymock.jar`
library
to your classpath.
	
You also
need to download the
http://objenesis.googlecode.com/svn/docs/download.html[Objenesis]
and
http://cglib.sourceforge.net/[Cglib]
libraries
and add these
`jars`
to your classpath.
